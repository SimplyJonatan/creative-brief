💡 Simply Piano Angles

5 angles from today's intelligence
Angle 01 — Inspired by Duolingo's Narrative Format
Give Simply Piano a Story Arc
Duolingo's biggest win wasn't an ad — it was a narrative people wanted to follow. Simply Piano has a natural story: the app was literally designed to be "simple." That's a personality, not just a product name. Build a character or persona around it — the patient teacher, the app that never judges, the one that waited for you to come back.
"You came back. We saved your spot. Let's pick up where you left off."
Signal: Duolingo "Death of Duo" — 144M views, narrative > product demo
Angle 02 — Counter Flowkey's "Never Too Late"
Own the 50+ Moment — But Do It Better
Flowkey uses "never too late" as a generic message. SP can own this segment with specificity: you have the time now, you have the brain, and this was literally designed so you don't need to be tech-savvy. Show people who look aspirational (under 40 visually), playing songs from the 70s–80s. Don't say "seniors" — say "this is your season."
"You spent 40 years wishing you played piano. You now have the time. This is how it's done in 2026."
Signal: Flowkey adult messaging + 50+ tech adoption surge (90% smartphone ownership in 2025)
Angle 03 — Exploit the Andromeda Shift
Build a Creative Library, Not One Hero Ad
With Meta's Andromeda update, targeting is dead — creative is the signal. SP needs 10–20 variants testing different hooks, tones, and angles simultaneously. The algorithm finds the audience; your job is to give it enough creative diversity to work with. One great ad is not enough in 2026. A library of 15 authentic variants is the new media buy.
Brief: 3 hooks × 3 formats × 2 audiences = 18 variants from one shoot day
Signal: Meta Andromeda algorithm — creative = targeting, UGC wins, static drives 60-70% conversions
Angle 04 — Ride the TikTok Piano Wave
Hijack Trending Songs as Ad Hooks
TikTok's #piano tag is organic gold. Viral piano content (trending song tutorials, "learn in 30 days") consistently outperforms on TikTok. SP should run ads that look like organic piano content — someone learning a trending song, showing progress, using the app naturally. The hook IS the song, not the app pitch. CPM is 3–4x cheaper than Meta.
"I learned [viral TikTok song] in 2 weeks with Simply Piano. Here's day 1 vs day 14."
Signal: TikTok #piano organic trends, $2.60–$6.60 CPM vs Meta $9–$15
Angle 05 — Fill Yousician's Gap
Emotional Over Gamified — The Anti-Game Angle
Yousician's positioning is heavily gamified. That leaves a clear gap: adults (especially 35+) don't want to feel like they're playing a video game — they want to feel like they're actually learning a real skill. SP's angle: "This isn't a game. This is the real thing. You'll actually play piano." Position against the toy-like feel of competitors and own the "serious beginner" space.
"Not a game. Not a toy. Simply Piano teaches you to actually play — the real songs, the right way."
Signal: Yousician gap analysis — strong on gamification, weak on emotional credibility for adults 35+

📈 Platform Trends This Week

What's shifting across Meta, TikTok & YouTube
🤖
Meta Andromeda: Creative = Targeting
Meta's new algorithm uses ad creative to determine audience — not the other way around. Narrow targeting is obsolete. Diverse creative libraries win. This is the single biggest paid social shift since iOS 14.
⚡ Action: Brief 15+ creative variants per campaign
🎵
TikTok Piano is Organically Viral
#piano is trending consistently. "Learn this song in 30 days" and prodigy content drive high watch time. Trending songs in Nov–Dec correlate with piano search peaks — high intent window for SP.
⚡ Action: Tap trending songs as ad hooks on TikTok
📱
50+ Audience is Fully Digital
Adults 50+ now have 90% smartphone ownership (up from 55% in 2016). 71% bought tech in 2025. They are NOT the laggards anymore. AARP data shows travel, learning, and wellness apps growing fastest in this demo.
⚡ Action: Increase 50+ budget allocation — audience is ready
🎭
Narrative Ads Outperform Product Demos
Duolingo proved it: a story people follow earns more media than a campaign you pay for. App ads that build narrative arcs (day 1 → day 30, before → after, "I almost quit but…") generate far higher completion rates and shares.
⚡ Action: Test a "journey" format — follow one learner over 30 days
🎬
UGC-Style Outperforms Polished Production
Across Meta and TikTok, raw authentic video (phone shot, natural lighting, real reactions) beats studio production in 2026. The algorithm rewards content that looks native to the feed, not like an ad.
⚡ Action: Brief UGC-style "real user" formats alongside polished ads
📊
Static Ads Still Drive 60–70% of Meta Conversions
Despite the video trend, static image ads remain the #1 conversion driver on Meta. Don't over-invest in video at the expense of static. A strong static hook + clear CTA still outperforms most video in direct response.
⚡ Action: Always include strong static variants in every test batch
